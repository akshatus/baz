Chto nughno znaty pro appendicit?

Chto nughno znaty pro appendicit?

Appendicit-bұl ghіtі қabynuy cherveobraznogo tәrіzdі өsіndі. Klinikada appendicit boluy mүmkіn өtkіr, bіraқ patologiyalyқ anatomiya keyde sozylmaly appendicit.

Bұl өte tanymal taқyryp arasynda dәrіgerler, studentter-medikter de, tіptі basқa mamandyқ, өytkenі, bұl auru өte ghiі kezdesedі bіzdің tanystarynyң, tuystarynyң, mүmkіn, bіreu өzі osy belgіmen.

Eger dәrіgerlerіmen ghәne studenttermen medicinalyқ ghoғary oқu Oryndarynyң barlyқ tүsіnіktі bolsa, қalғan halyқ әreң tүsіnedі barlyқ kүrdelіlіgіn osy patologiya. Әrine, barlyғy bіledі,bұl қauіptі,bіraқ men үshіn sonshalyқty қauіptі...

Mіne, қarapayym adamdarғa men bara ghatyrmyn tүsіndіr shattan, osy neduga.

Bastaymyn өzіnің bayandau otyryp, auyrsynudyң payda қazіrdің өzіnde aurudyң bastapқy satysyndaғy. Auru emes, mіndettі tүrde oң ghaқ myқyn aymaғynda ornalasқan otrostok, kerіsіnshe, ol payda bolady epigastriy aymaғynyң 80% ghaғdaylarda. Sodan keyіn auru bіrtіndep tөmen tүsedі-da oң ghaқ bөlіmderі іshtі.

Әr adamnyң shegі auyrsynu sezіmtaldyғy әrtүrlі ghәne bіr қatty uayymdaydy, appendicit, mіne, basynan-barlar tөmen auyrsynu shegі; ghәne basқa da-ghoғary shegі, mүmkіn eshteңe sezіnuі nemese sezіnuge tek ghaғymsyz tүysіk.

Kelesі soraқysy құbylys-қyzba. Ol kөpshіlіgіnde adamdar. Ghәne dene temperaturasy ghetedі, tіptі, 38 gradus ghekelegen ghaғdaylarda.

Osydan keyіn payda bolady ghalpy ulanu aғza ghәne dispepsiyalyқ bұzylys bұzylular, yaғni bұzylular as қorytu. Adamdar zardap shegedі құlyp, nemese kerіsіnshe - keybіr bayқaluy mүmkіn sұyyқ nәghіs; құsu, bastyң auruy, auyzdyң құrғauy, shөldeu bұl intoksikaciya simptomdary.

Ghoғaryda sipattalғan tiptіk aғymy appendicittің, bіraқ ұmytuғa bolmaydy, bұl bar ghәne tiptіk nysany. Ol kөbіnese byvaet ghyldyң ghyly mezgіlіnde nemese balalar. Bұl ghaғdayda barlyқ belgіlerі boluy mүmkіn nemese sglagheny, ne bіr belgіlerі ghoқ.

Sondyқtan saқ bolyңyz ghәne өz densaulyғyңyzdy kүtіңіz. Eger simptomdar payda ghedel appendicittің ghedel tүrde shaқyryңyz ghedel medicinalyқ kөmek.
