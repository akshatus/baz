Nege Nabiullina satty obligaciyalardy 50 mlrd $?

Sәuіr ayynda memleket basshysy bіzdің BҚ ghastaғy rasprodaghu egemendіk bondov 50 mlrd dollarғa қysқartyp, bіzdің investiciyalar AҚSh-tyң memlekettіk қaryzy 2 esege өstі ghәne bұl tek bіr ay)))).

Kөptegen қoyylady mәsele ne үshіn solay ghasady bіzdің BҚ?

Өzіnің aytuynsha, memleket-bұl әrtaraptandyru maқsatynda halyқaralyқ rezervterdі eskere otyryp, barlyқ tәuekelder қazіr bolyp ghatқan әlemdіk arenada. Sauda soғysy ghinaydy aynalymy ghәne қarsy sankciyalar bіzdің kompaniyalar ghalғastyruda risovatysya mәdeniet үyі ghөndelude. Shamasy, Tramp tүspek aқyl men қalaydy barlyқ pokazaty kіm mұnda Әkem.

Bұl bіzdің BҚ ұlғaytudy ghalғastyruda altyn-valyuta rezervterі ұlғaytyp, altynnyң үlesіn aktivіnde іs ghүzіnde 10 esege, al bolady bөlіgі dollarғa ғana satuғa өte tiіmdі baғamen ghіbere otyryp, қanday basқa maқsattarғa ghәne әleumettіk sheshіmder.

Barlyқ kөrsetedі, bұl ghaһandyқ әlemdegі қarym-қatynastar tvoritysya өte қiyn ghaғday. Iіsі shielenіstі ghay ғana қalқyp ghүrgendey қazіrdің өzіnde auada ghәne bіzdің memleket sheshtі zapasatysya "tushenkoy" ghұmysta emes baru, ghaғdaydy қalay Deripaska. Iә, bәrі dұrys BҚ ghіberedі қazіr barlyқ rezervter қaluғa әlemdіk oyynda dollarymen, bіraқ eger mұzdatu bіzdің aktivterdің қalmas s ghaғatyn popoy.

ZVR (altyn-valyutalyқ rezervter) қazіr қol ghetkіzdіk mөlsherіn 461 mlrd $.

Ghalғastyramyz ghaғdaydy baқylap қaray, bіraқ barlyқ osy іs-әreketter bіzdің BҚ aytady dүniesіn әzіrge sayasatta pahnet, al anyқ dayyndyқ bastaldy. Saқ bolyңyz ghәne ұstaңyz barlyқ ghinaқ bіr valyutada.

Ұmytpaymyz қoyuғa laykter ghәne podpisatysya na moy kanal!
