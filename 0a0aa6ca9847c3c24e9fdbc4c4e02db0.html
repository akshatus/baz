Dep esepteymіn bar barlyқ emes tolstety, eger kaloriya ghұmsauғa қaraғanda olardyң syedaty. Bұl men үshіn basty қaғida. Bұl eң basty dieta, ol ghұmys іsteydі.

Әrine, қazіr barlyқ otyrғanda үy, қozғalys belsendіlіgі tөmendegen, kөp ghүruge, seruendeuge, ghүgіruge, sportpen aynalysu oңay bolғan ghoқ. Ghaқsy eger үyde bar trenagherler, ghүgіru gholy, sheңber ghәne sekіrgіsh, sіz үlken үyі bar oқu orny.

Bіraқ kөptegen tұrady shaғyn pәterlerde, onda ghүgіruge ghәne sportpen aynalysuғa өte problemaly bolyp tabylady.

Sondyқtan, eger barlyқ mүmkіndіgі bar - aynalysyңyz dene belsendіlіgі. Zaryadtau taңerteң, prisedaniya, oyyndar balalarmen it ghayau, tolassyz tүleu, damylsyz.

Analog dene belsendіlіgі - bұl kez-kelgen ghұmys ghәne үyde. Internette tіptі bar keste, қanday қyzmet қansha kaloriya ghaғady. Ghuyp, edenderdі ghuu ydys-ayaқ, tereze, ghasauғa auystyrudy pәterde, tүskі as dayyndau - bұl өte ghaқsy ghұmys іsteydі, pohudety.

Shaқyryldy bolady өzіn shektemeu. Ghaқsy bar osy sharany, 5 ret az mөlshermen қaraғanda 3 ese, bіraқ үlken tarelkami.

Signal ekenіn syty tүsedі miy arқyly 20 minuttan keyіn, sіz poeli. Elestetіp kөrіңіzshі қansha bolady barlyғy gheuge 20 minut.

Tek keybіr adamdar otyrady osynday қataң dietah, onda tek kurinuyu grudku, salat ghapyraғy ghәne ayran. Bіrіnshіden, bұl auyr, psihologiyalyқ, barlyқ uaқytta gheuge keledі, onda dәmdі keledі, өzіne үnemі shekteu. Ekіnshіden, olarda ghiі salmaғy nemese orynda tұrғan ketedі nemese өte bayau.

Ghәne қaғida "saғat 18.00-den Keyіn emes, bar- alasyң әrқashan stroynym", menіңshe, ghұmys іsteydі әrқashan. Ghaқsy emes, toyyp tamaқtanbau, tүnі, bұl shyndyқ, bіraқ eger sіz loghitesy kesh bolady gheuge soңғy ret 3 saғat bұryn ұyқy emes, den 18.00-ge deyіn.

Bolady bәrі bar, bіraқ bіrіnshі ghartysyna kүn. Yaғni taңerteң bolady өzіne, tіptі bіr tіlіm tortika, pechenye nemese өzge de tәttіler, tіptі өrbidі may.
