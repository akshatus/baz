Dieta aydaһar: "ereghenің 8-tamaқtanu Bryusa Li

Aty aңyzғa aynalғan adam, ghauyngerlіk өner, nұsқaushy, filosof, bishі, akter ghәne reghisser Bryus Li bolғan idealdy tұlғa. Terі astynda қytay ghauynger bіrde-tamshy artyқ maydy.

Bryus li қol ghetkіzdі osynday nәtighenің arқasynda dұrys tamaқtanu ghәne iznuritelynym ghattyғular. Bүkіl өzіnің қysқa, bіraқ өte gharқyn өmіr ghauyngerlіk өner ұstanғan қataң dieta turaly, onyң aytylady maқalada.

Procesіnde қalyptastyru өz mәzіr, Li ghүrgіzgen eksperimentter tүrlі tүrlerі өnіmder. Ghauyngerlіk өnerdің klassikalyқ tүsterdің mәzіr bodibilder қytay taғamdary.

Nәtighede "dieta" aydaһar, ol segіz ereghesіn. Eger sіz olardy ұstanuғa, onda sіz eshқashan artyқ may.

1. Aulaқ ұn
Bұl gherde atap өtu maңyzdy, Bryus emes vycherkival өz mәzіr barlyқ ұnnan ghasalғan өnіmder, ol tyrysty, olar terіs emes bolady. Kөbіnese ghauynger el үnnan ghasalғan қonaққa, қaghettіlіgі tanytuғa sypayylyқ k ielerіne үy.

Bіraқ bіr negіzgі қaғidalaryn ghәne onyң dieta glasilo dep organizmge қaghetі ghoқ ghіberuge kaloriya emes, paydasy denege taraydy.

2. Ghidekterdі paydalanu aldynda қytay as
Bұl deydі onyң bar tүrlі kemіrgіshter ghәne basқa mәnderdің, olar taratatyn virustar. Қytay mәzіrі өte obshirno ghәne onda үlken sany bar paydaly taғam.

Bryus erekshe bөlse siyr da ustrichnom tұzdyқta ghәne soyalyқ tofu іrіmshіgі. Bұl ydys-ayaқ vkusny ie kөp aқuyz.

Ayta keteyіk, Li өte ұnady steykter ghәne pechenku, bіraқ әrқashan dep sanaғan europalyқ as basa nazar audarylady, may, sondyқtan zloupotreblyal osy taғam. Sonymen қatar kinoghұldyz ghiі el kүrіsh, өytkenі bұl өnіm құramynda sany kөp, kөmіrsular ghәne vitaminder tobynyң B.

3. Aulaқ sүt өnіmderіn
Bryus Li іs ghүzіnde vycherknul sүt өnіmderі dietalar. Ghauynger tұtynғan sүt tek құrғaқ kүyіnde kezіnde ony aralastyru belkovym kokteylem.

4. Az mөlsherde, kөp rettіk tamaқtanu
Қazіrgі kezde bұl әdіs өte tanymal! Қytay akter әrқashan onyң ұstanғan. Mәnі өte қarapayym: gheңіz ghiі, bіraқ az, aғzaғa қaghettіlіgі ghoқ tapsyrys mol bolatyn keyіnge maylar pro қory. Sіz kүtu kerek әzіrge ornaydy ashyғu sezіmі.

Boryus delil tamaқ іshuge bes tәsіlderdі, bіrkelkі bөle otyryp, olardyң kүnі boyy. Porciyanyң kіshkentay, al arasyndaғy aralyқtarda olardyң tәsіlderіn ghauyngerlіk өner tұtynғan ghaңa pіsken ghemіster.

5. Іshіңіz proteinovye kokteylyder

Kinoghұldyz kүndelіktі tұtynғan aқuyzdy susyn, ol tұrdy, құrғaқ sүt, ghұmyrtқa, proroschennoy biday, gherghaңғaқ mayy, may, banan, syra ashytқysyn, inozitola ghәne lecitin alynғan.

Sportshy eksperimentiroval өnіmderіmen aralastyru arқyly olardy blendere, sodan keyіn dobavlyal kіlegey. Kөptegen kөzderі bekіtedі, bұl ghұmyrtқa bұl drakonyevskiy kokteyly popadali tіkeley sіlteme sүyegі қosylғan.

6. Қabyldaңyz taғamdyқ қospalar.
Aydaһar boldy ghaқtaushysy қabyldau үlken sanyn taғamdyқ қospalar. Belgіlі mәzіr ghauynger қatysқandar: vitamin C, tүyіrshіkter lecitin alynғan, bal ara ұyasy tozaң, vitamin E, sirop, itmұryn, sonday-aқ ghemіster aceroly.

7. Қabyldaңyz kөmіrsular
Belsendіrek adam kөp қuat ghұmsaytynyn, tiіsіnshe, ony toltyru қaghet қosymsha kөzі. Үshіn Bryusa osynday kөzі bolatyn kөmіrsular. Sportshy үnemі tұtynғan kokteli ghaңa pіsken ghemіster ghәne kөkөnіster.

Sүyіktі kokteylі kinozvezdy tұrdy kelesі bіrқatar өnіmder: sәbіz, baldyrkөk, alma, banan, aқghelken. Kөkөnіster men ghemіster propuskalisy arқyly blender, sodan keyіn poluchalsya tamasha ghұmsaғy bar shyryn. Atap өtu maңyzdy, bұl sәbіz atқardy basym rөl chempiondyқ kokteylyah, sebebі onyң әrқashan kөp boldy, 50%.

8. Bal gheңіz, ghenysheny
Ghauyngerlіk өner sengen ghenysheny ghabdyқtaydy adam aғzasy energiyasymen "Yan", ol terіnі kүsh, қanaynalymdy ghaқsartady. Paydasy turaly bal menіңshe қiyn. Bryus paydalandy, bұl өnіmder үshіn "zaryadtau".

Қorytyndylay kele, tyrysamyz ghasauғa үlgі racion "Aydaһar":

  • Taңғy as: shaғyn porciya myusley s suhofruktami, bіr stakan apelysin shyryny.
  • Ekіnshі taңғy as: protein kokteylі (қaraңyz: 5-tarmaқ).
  • Tүskі as: Shay, et, kүrіsh ghәne kөkөnіs.
  • Tүsten keyіngі as: Aқuyz kokteyly, shay
  • Keshkі as: makarondar, tauyқ nemese teңіz өnіmderі, shyryn

Әrine, eң bastysy, ereghe mynada: ұstanu қaghet қataң reghim. Ghalpy, dieta өte қarapayym, alayda, bar azdaғan kemshіlіkter siyaқty қymbattyғy keybіr taғamdyқ қospalardy ghәne shektelgen tіzіmі қabyldanatyn өnіmder.

Al osy mәsele boyynsha oylaysyz sіz?

Ghauynger, mіndettі tүrde pishi өz pіkіrіn eskertuler, sonday-aқ stavy barmaғyn ghoғary ghәne podpisyvaysya na kanal, senің қoldau -ghұtym taza aua үshіn bіzdің arna!
